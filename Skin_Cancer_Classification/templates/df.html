Dermatofibroma (DF)

What you need to know about this diagnosis

What is Dermatofibroma?

Dermatofibroma is a common benign skin growth that appears as a small, firm bump, often on the legs. It's composed of fibrous tissue and is harmless.

Key Characteristics:

  • Small, firm bump under the skin
  • Color ranges from pink to brown
  • Dimple sign - dimples when pinched
  • Most common on legs, but can appear anywhere
  • Usually 3-10mm in size

Good News

Our model has detected a dermatofibroma, which is a benign skin growth. These are harmless and don't require treatment unless they bother you.

Next Steps:

  • No treatment needed unless it's bothersome
  • Monitor for any changes (rare)
  • See a doctor if it grows rapidly or changes color
  • Can be removed surgically if desired
