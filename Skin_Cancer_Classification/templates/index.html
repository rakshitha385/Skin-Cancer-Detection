About Skin Cancer

What is Skin Cancer?

Skin cancer occurs when abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in the skin layers. The main types are basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and melanoma.

Warning Signs

Look for changes in size, shape, or color of moles or skin lesions. Remember the ABCDE rule for melanoma detection.

Prevention

Use sunscreen, avoid peak sun hours, wear protective clothing, and regularly check your skin for changes.
